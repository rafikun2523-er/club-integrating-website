Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BAUET Computer Society

Who can join the club?

Only students of BAUET are eligible to join this club. Beginners and tech enthusiasts from our university are most welcome.

How to join the computer club?

Click on 'Log In', then select 'Register Here' and provide the required information. Joining is completely free of cost.

Do I need prior experience in programming?

No prior skills or experience are required! This club welcomes all BAUET students whether you are a complete beginner or already proficient in technology.

When does this club hold its competitions?

Our club organizes competitions every semester. You have to be a member to get announcements for upcoming contests or events.

Can I participate in events without being a member?

Yes, you can join events even without membership. However, we recommend becoming a member to enjoy full benefits and receive updates.

What benefits do members get?

Club members gain exclusive access to workshops, hands-on projects, competitions and networking opportunities while staying updated on all club events.

Do we get certificates?

Active participants receive certificates for competitions and workshops.

Can I join multiple clubs?

Yes, you can join other clubs while being part of our computer club.

With whom can I contact for club-related queries?

Use the Contact section or email computer.society.bauet@gmail.com for any inquiries.

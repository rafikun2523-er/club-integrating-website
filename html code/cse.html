FAQ

  • How to join the computer club? Register online via the Join Us section and pay the membership fee.
  • When are the next competitions? Competitions are held each semester; check the Events section for updates.
  • How to access online resources? Log in to the portal to access tutorials, code samples, and guides.
  • Can I participate as a beginner? Yes! Beginners are welcome, and mentorship is provided.
  • Do we get certificates? Active participants receive certificates for competitions and workshops.
  • Are workshops free? Some workshops are free; others may require a nominal fee. Check announcements.
  • Can I join multiple clubs? Yes, you can join other clubs while being part of our computer club.
  • How to contact the club for queries? Use the Contact section or email computerclub@bauet.ac.bd for inquiries.

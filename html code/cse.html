FAQ

  • Who can join the club? Only students of BAUET are eligible to join this club. Beginners and tech enthusiasts from our university are most welcome
  • How to join the computer club? Click on 'Log In', then select 'Register Here' and provide the required information. Joining is completely free of cost.
  • Do I need prior experience in programming or computers to join the club? No prior skills or experience are required! This club welcomes all BAUET students whether you are a complete beginner or already proficient in technology
  • When does this club hold its competitions? Our club organizes competitions every semester. You have to be a member of our club to get the announcements for our upcoming contests or events.
  • Can I participate in events without being a member? Yes, you can join events even without membership. However, we recommend becoming a member to enjoy full benefits, receive updates and have a smoother event experience.
  • What benefits do members get? Club members gain exclusive access to workshops, hands-on projects, competitions and networking opportunities. Membership allows you to learn, collaborate and grow your skills in a supportive environment while staying updated on all club events and activities.
  • Do we get certificates? Active participants receive certificates for competitions and workshops.
  • Can I contribute to projects even if I am a newcomer? Yes! All newcomers are welcome to contribute to projects, regardless of prior experience. The club encourages learning and collaboration, so you can participate and grow your skills while working on real projects.
  • Can I join multiple clubs? Yes, you can join other clubs while being part of our computer club.
  • With whom can I contact for club-related queries? Use the Contact section or email computer.society.bauet@gmail.com for inquiries.

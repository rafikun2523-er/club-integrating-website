About Our Club

BAUET Computer Society — committed to building digital Bangladesh through innovation, coding and leadership.

Introduction

The importance of various clubs besides class education is immense for the development of creative education and talent of the university as well as for the development of the nation. BAUET Computer Society was founded with a commitment to build a digital Bangladesh because the future of the country depends on the talent and values of the young society.

This club will increase the efficiency of its members, pave the way for employment and work with the great vow of building ideal people. The club consists of a total of 13 sub-committees covering Software Development, Programming, Web Development, Cyber Security, Research, Hardware, AI & Robotics and more.

Membership Requirements

Must be a student of BAUET
CGPA ≥ 3.00 in any semester
Completely free — no joining fee
No monthly or annual subscription
Fill the prescribed registration form
Open to all departments of BAUET
Students with CGPA below 3.00 in any semester are not eligible for membership.

Rules & Regulations

01

All members are bound to abide by all rules of the club.

02

Members must maintain control of the club.

03

Every effort should be made to take part in all the events of the club.

04

Every effort should be made to engage in voluntary work as required, as advised by the event coordinator.

05

Members may not engage in any activity that would be shameful to the club.

06

If there is any concern, the head of the committee should be informed. The head will inform the executive committee and necessary action will be taken.

07

All members must attend each meeting. If a member is absent, he must show cause. If he does not attend two consecutive meetings without showing cause, the executive committee will take necessary action.

08

If any member breaks the rules of the club or tries to create chaos, he will be immediately dismissed from the club. No dismissed student will be allowed to participate in any club activities or events.

09

If any member misbehaves with any other member or executive member, he shall be dismissed from the club.

10

Strict action will be taken against anyone who tries to cause problems during various events.

11

The members have to fulfill the responsibilities assigned to them within the stipulated time. Otherwise the authorities will take necessary action.

12

At the monthly meeting, the members of the club should present the progress of the work to the members of the committee.

13

No assets of the Club may be used for personal or other purposes.

14

If the members of the club need to work in the lab, they have to maintain the register book of the lab.

15

The Executive Committee has the power to add, subtract, change or add new provisions at any time as it deems necessary.

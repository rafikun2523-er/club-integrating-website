About Unified Club Portal

A digital platform built to connect BAUET students with university clubs, events, and campus life — all in one place.

Our Mission
Why we built this portal.
🎯
Digitize Campus Life
Bring all BAUET clubs into one unified digital space for easy discovery and management.
🤝
Connect Students
Help students find their community, join clubs that match their passion, and build friendships.
🚀
Grow Together
Empower club leaders with tools to manage events, members, and announcements effectively.
Our Journey
How the portal grew over the years.
2015
Portal Founded
The Unified Club Portal was launched with 2 clubs and 50 initial members at BAUET.
2018
Expanded to 5 Clubs
Cultural, Photography, and Sports clubs joined, growing the member base to 300+.
2022
Online Event System
Introduced digital event registration and real-time notification for all club members.
2026
Full Digital Relaunch
Complete portal redesign with admin panel, member management, and 500+ active members.
